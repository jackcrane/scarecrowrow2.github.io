SCARECROW ROW

OTTO ARMLEDER MEMORIAL PARK







Scarecrow Row is a fun, fall art installation of scarecrows set among the winding paths of Otto Armleder Park, located on the eastside of Cincinnati. This is our third year, and we are so happy to be working with Hamilton County Great Parks & Cincinnati Recreation again.
