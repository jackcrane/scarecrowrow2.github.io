SCARECROW ROW

9/28/2020 - 10/24/2020

OTTO ARMLEDER MEMORIAL PARK


+

Scarecrow Row is a fun, fall art installation of scarecrows set among the winding paths of Otto Armleder Park, located on the eastside of Cincinnati. This is our fourth year, but our first year to be raising funds for Madi's house, serving young adults batting addiction and mental health illness in the Greater Cincinnati Area.
