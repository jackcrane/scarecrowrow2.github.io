About Scarecrow Row

Scarecrow Row is a fun fall installation of unique and creative scarecrows set along the 2-mile bike and walking trail of Otto Armleder Park, located on Cincinnati’s eastside next to Lunken Airport and Playfield.

Individuals, organizations and businesses submit one of a kind, themed scarecrows, that will amuse and entertain visitors throughout the month of October.

Scarecrow Row is a proud supporter of Madi’s House, a relapse prevention and mental health community center, serving young adults battling addiction and mental health illness in the greater Cincinnati area.

For each scarecrow that is submitted we are hoping to raise $250 for Madi’s House.

You can donate individually, or create a team to collectively raise the money. Name your team, and have your members place that name in the dropdown space provided. We’ll track your team’s donations and give you a final total.

It has been a rough year for some. If you would like to submit a scarecrow only, we would gladly accept it with deep appreciation!

Help make Scarecrow Row for Madi’s House a success, so that we can best serve the young adults who battle their illnesses every day!
