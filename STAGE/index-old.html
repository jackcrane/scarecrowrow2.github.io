SCARECROW ROW

10/3/2021 - 10/30/2021

OTTO ARMLEDER MEMORIAL PARK


Scarecrow Row is a fun, fall art installation of scarecrows set among the winding paths of Otto Armleder Park, located on the eastside of Cincinnati.

This is our 5 year anniversary, and our 2nd year to be raising funds for Madi's house, serving young adults batting addiction and mental health illness in the Greater Cincinnati Area.
